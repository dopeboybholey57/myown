Dr. Srinivas Vudathala

Chief Operations Officer
Dr. Srinivas has a strong cross-functional domain expertise of 26 years encompassing Diagnostics, Genomics (Next-Generation Sequencing), Bioinformatics, Proteomics, Automation and IT (SAP). He has been instrumental in designing, developing and implementing State-of-Art Next-Generation Sequencing and Diagnostics labs in various organizations. He has been trained in Laboratory Management and Internal Audit – ISO/IEC 17025:2017 and Quality Management Systems and NABL 112 – ISO/IEC 15189 2012 and has the credit of getting many NABL accreditations. A strong proponent of NGS-based Diagnostics, Dr. Srinivas has successfully launched Oncomine Cancer Panels, Reproductive Health Panels – NIPT / PGS, Inherited Disease Panels and Clinical Exomes. He has publications in National and International journals.

Dr. Nina Dutta Roy

Lab Director
Dr. Nina Dutta Roy has vast experience in medical diagnostic field of more than 38 years. She acquired MBBS degree from the prestigious King George’s Medical College Lucknow in 1975. Thereafter, she joined Army Medical Corps and acquired MD (Microbiology) from Armed Forces Medical College, Pune (affiliated to Pune university) and obtained 1st rank. She was selected as graded specialist in Pathology and Microbiology. She has a rich teaching experience as an Asst & Associate Professor in Microbiology at AFMC. After taking premature retirement from Army Medical Corps in 1999, she has been associated with various reputed Corporate Hospitals and Diagnostics Laboratories as Lab Director and Transfusion Medicine In-charge. She is an NABL Technical Assessor in the field of Microbiology and Serology.
